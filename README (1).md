# 🎓 Career Buddy – Mentor Agent

Career Buddy is your AI-powered productivity coach. Just enter your weekly goals and tasks — and Career Buddy gives you:

- ✅ Auto-generated Weekly Plans with Rewards
- 🎯 Motivation & Streaks to Stay on Track
- 🧠 Firebase-powered Recall of Previous Tasks
- 🔍 Experimental RAG-based Career Suggestions (DeepSeek)
